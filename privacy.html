Privacy Policy — AI Shortcut Studio

Last updated: July 9, 2026

AI Shortcut Studio is a private, single-user tool used solely to publish content to the Instagram account @ai_shortcut_daily, operated by the app's owner.

What data we collect

None. This app does not collect, store, sell, or share any personal data from any third party. It is not offered to the public and has no users other than its owner.

How the app works

The app uses the Instagram API (with Instagram Login) to publish the owner's own original content to the owner's own Instagram account. Access tokens are stored privately on the owner's local machine and are never shared.

Data deletion

Since no third-party data is collected, there is nothing to delete. To revoke the app's access to the Instagram account, remove it under Instagram Settings > Website permissions > Apps and websites.
